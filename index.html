L and LL words


Convention/Grammar

Single "L" Words:

  • These words have just one "L."
  • Examples:Look,Lost,Leaf
  • Pronunciation: We say the "L" sound softly, like in "Look" (luhk).

Double "LL" Words:

  • These words have two "L's" together.
  • Examples:Still,Well,All
  • Pronunciation: The "LL" sound is a bit stronger, like in "Still" (st-ihl).

Remember: Single "L" is like a soft, quick sound, and double "LL" makes the sound stronger and longer.
